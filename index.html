PoniGame

Build friendships like never before.

PoniGame aspires to be a free-to-play Sandbox MMO game that combines multiplayer RPG components with socialization features, to allow players to collaborate with each other to advance their friendship levels.

Players will also be given tools to interact with the world around them, from levitating objects through magix spells, to the ability to build structures and fly!

Development is in full swing!
